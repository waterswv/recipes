Roasted Hablibut

Ingredients:
- 6-8oz. fresh halibut
- 1 lemon sliced
- 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
- 1 tsp thyme
- 1/3 cup of plain greek yogurt
- 1 tbsp hot sauce [optional]

Preheat oven to broil - 500 degrees

Mix thyme, yogurt, & hot sauce together in bowl. Set aside

Brush olive oil on the tob of the fish. Then spread an even layer of mixture on top of the fish. 

Lay the sliced lemons on a baking pan and place fish on top. 

Bake fish 10-15 min depending on thickness. 

Enjoy!
